Alex Kirkham

Bio

Alex Kirkham currently works at the North East Ambulance Service as a Senior Information Analyst. He works with complex data covering the regional 999 and 111 services, analysing call level data for local and national applications and developing reporting functionality to drive understanding and performance of the service.

Prior to starting his career in the NHS, he completed a PhD at Bangor University (Experimental and Cognitive Psychology) under the supervision of Dr. Paloma Mari-Beffa, and worked to increase understanding of verbalisations in cognitive control and task-switching. Following this he worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of York with Prof. Steven Tipper on an ESRC funded project entitled 'Visuomotor states of an observer influence person perception: The critical role of emotion'.
